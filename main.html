At Bird Construction, we specialize in roofing, siding, and interior renovation services, with over a decade of hands-on experience serving homeowners across Queens County and Nassau County. Our reputation is built on a foundation of trust, craftsmanship, and a commitment to protecting and enhancing your home.

Roofing has been at the heart of our business for more than 10 years. From small repairs to full roof replacements, we deliver high-quality work using durable materials and detailed workmanship that stands the test of time. We don’t just patch problems—we provide long-term solutions.

Beyond roofing, we bring new life to homes through expert siding installation and repair, and interior renovation projects like kitchen makeovers, bathroom upgrades, and basement transformations. Every job is treated with respect, care, and professionalism—because your home is your most valuable investment.

With 30+ five-star reviews and a fully insured team, our clients consistently praise our reliability, communication, cleanliness, and high standards of work. We’re here to deliver honest results with clear timelines and affordable pricing.
